Installments

Installment by credit card (Visa purchases)

Is an installment system available to customers who carry a credit card (Visa Purchases) for customers of :

1. Al Ahli Bank
2. Misr bank
3. QNB Bank

Advantages of installment through credit cards

  1. Installment for 12 months on all household appliances and some electrical appliances that are advertised through our
    Facebook page
  2. Products can be ordered by applying Hyper Techno or by telephone and making use of the payment service upon
    receipt through credit cards .

Installment through banks

This system is available to public and private sector employees and pensioners for a maximum of 60 years

Paperwork required of
staff for submission to banks

  1. A doucement with salary Issued by
    the employer
  2. A copy of I.D
  3. New rent or proprietary Recipt

Pensioners

  1. Statement of pension status
  2. Picture of a I.D
  3. Copy of the guarantor card
  4. Delivery of modern facilities

Installment through Contact

Is a system of installment available to employees in all government and private bodies and pensioners for the age of
not more than 60 years and also for the owners of shops and small projects .

Paperwork required of
staff for submission to Contact

  1. A doucment with salary Issued by
    the employer + Any proof of other
    income
  2. A copy of I.D
  3. Copy of the guarantor card
  4. New rent or proprietary Recipt

Paperworkrequired for
owners of shops and projects

  2. A doucement with salary Issued by
    the employer + Any proof of other
    income
  3. A copy of I.D
  4. Copy of the guarantor card
  5. New rent or proprietary Recipt

Important Notes

  1. When applying for an installment with Connecticut, a non-refundable inquiry fee of $ 100 is paid.
  2. There should be no late loans with the banks until the rejection process is completed on the installment request .
